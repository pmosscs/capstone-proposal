1. THE PROJECT
    A "driving for dollars" website. 

2. THE FEATURES 
    This website allows you to add potential investment houses you find in 
    your town to a list. You can manage your list of houses and mark them as 
    contacted, or upadate their status like: "seller uninterested", "waiting 
    on inspection", or "offer extented/accepted". - potential feature: exporting a list of all addresses and names for a letter campaign.
